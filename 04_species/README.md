# Species

Status: EMERGING

- **Humans** — baseline population; subject to the Chimeran Flu outbreak at Cape Wipeout.
- **Chimeras** — emerge from Era 3 institutional experimentation ("for medicine,
  adaptation, military advantage, and survival"); explicitly "new beings, not monsters."
  Era 7's founding line: "They called us monsters. We became the care they were afraid
  to give." Tallai/Talla is a chimera care specialist. By the Kingdom Age (Era 15),
  chimeras are understood well enough to be a distinct population alongside Cybernetic
  Houses and Pure Human Communities.
  - **Lion lineage** and **Jaguar lineage** (added 2026-06-25, per
    `UMADA_CANON_INDEX.md`) — the first and second successful chimera lineages,
    stabilized in the immediate aftermath of Cape Wipeout using Cindy's sediment. The
    Lion lineage stabilizes first; the Jaguar lineage works better but comes later.
    Whether Tallai, Q, or Quartz belong to either named lineage is not stated anywhere
    and is not assumed — see `01_canon/OPEN_QUESTIONS.md` (#31).
- **Cyborgs / Cybernetics** — begin in Era 9 (Emergency Cybernetics) as medical repair,
  accessibility, and survival tools; later become status systems. Nago signage notes
  must specify whether a sign is usable by humans, chimeras, cyborgs, or all.
- **S-Vector descendants** (added 2026-06-25, per `UMADA_CANON_INDEX.md`) — alien
  federation descendants per the new S-Vector House; connected to the new Quiet Arrival
  event (a peaceful interstellar federation arriving two years before Cindy, with some
  members settling on Earth). Whether this population is meant as a fourth distinct
  species category alongside humans/chimeras/cyborgs, or as a lineage within humans, is
  not stated. Substantially clarifies, but does not resolve, the previously-undefined
  `Era 2 — S-Vector` — see `01_canon/OPEN_QUESTIONS.md` (#35).

No physiological, cultural, or individual-chimera-variety detail exists beyond the above.
See `01_canon/CIVILIZATIONAL_LEDGER.md` (Eras 2, 3, 8, 9, 15) for the source eras.
