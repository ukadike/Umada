# Episode 01 — Cape Wipeout, Pt. 1 — Draft Script v1

Status: **DRAFT SCRIPT — first pass.** Generated from recovered canon, per
`docs/production/ROADMAP.md`. This is the first version of this episode written as a
script rather than a fact-compilation; see `11_episodes/EPISODE_01_CAPE_WIPEOUT.md` for
the underlying scaffold, which this draws from line for line.

**How this was built, and the one rule it follows:** every caption, title card, and line
of verbatim text below is either (a) quoted directly from the rescue animatic's
transcribed captions (`01_canon/VISUAL_CANON_REGISTRY.md`) or (b) quoted directly from
`01_canon/FOUNDING_MYTHS_AND_FIRST_LINES.md`, which is locked, first-class canon
preserved verbatim per `01_canon/SEED_CANON.md`'s poetry rule. Scene description is
written only where a fact is already locked (date, place, a character's locked
reference image, a role-fact). **No character in this episode has a locked spoken
voice** — every dossier says so explicitly (`02_characters/`) — so no character speaks a
written line anywhere in this draft. That is not an oversight: the one finished
narrative artifact this episode is built from, the animatic, also has no spoken
dialogue, only burned-in captions. Where a fact this script would need doesn't exist
yet, it is marked `[OPEN — #N]` inline rather than invented. Three such gaps were
surfaced for the first time while drafting this script and have been logged as new
entries in `01_canon/OPEN_QUESTIONS.md` (#32–#34, added 2026-06-23) — see the **Open
questions surfaced while drafting** section at the end of this file.

## EXT. FRIEDMANDOSTORP — AFTERMATH — DAY

Southern Cape corridor, near Cape Agulhas — colorful, weather-rich, painted; never
Arctic (`00_governance/CANON_STATUS.md`).

CAPTION: *"The town is in shards."*
CAPTION: *"The town is blown open."*

*(Verbatim, animatic 0:05–0:12.)*

CAPTION: *"Corrugated roofs. Cement dust. Clay. Sewage. Bread in the road."*

*(Verbatim, animatic 0:12–0:15.)*

Bread in the road is not set dressing chosen for this script — it is the literal locked
detail. Friedmandostorp's bread culture carries Nigerian-diaspora Agege-bread influence
(`01_canon/SEED_CANON.md`); the Bread Sign — an early Nago market sign — has its origin
at this town's bread stop, where Tallai is known before the blast.

CAPTION: *"A face first. Then the damage."*

*(Verbatim, animatic 0:15–0:21.)*

The face is Ada's. Per her locked reference (`assets/concept-art/ADA_CHARACTER_REFERENCE_v1.png`
and `02_characters/ADA_DOSSIER.md`): her hair, normally slicked back in a ponytail, is
mangled by the blast. She wears the woodland-camo uniform with its name tape, gold
collar insignia, wings/pilot-style badge, sleeve flag patch, web belt, sidearm, knife,
wristwatch, and the necklace Kassey gave her — a simple silver chain, a small solid heart
pendant. Her wasteland-aftermath state matches
`assets/concept-art/ADA_WASTELAND_REFERENCE_v1.png`.

CAPTION: *"Not dead. Not yet."*

*(Verbatim, animatic 0:21–0:26.)*

In this panel, a mark resembling the letters **"FFF"** is etched near the figures. It is
unexplained in the source material and is not interpreted here
(`01_canon/OPEN_QUESTIONS.md` #26).

CAPTION: *"And one name burning under all of it."*

*(Verbatim, animatic 0:26–0:30.)*

ON SCREEN: a torn military record. Stamped **CLEARANCE DENIED**. Legible fragments:
*"...VENTER"*, *"...STED KIA"* (most likely "LISTED KIA"), a descriptor line *"[C]HIMERA
SURVIVOR. SCIENTIST. EMPATH."* — Ada's own descriptors, not Kassey's
(`02_characters/KASSEY_DOSSIER.md`). Burned across the typed record, in a different,
glowing script: the name **Kassey**. Why his name burns across her record specifically
is a beat the animatic shows but does not explain, and this script does not explain it
either (`01_canon/OPEN_QUESTIONS.md` #30).

CAPTION: *"The road comes broken: paperwork, fire, betrayal — and Kassey."*

*(Verbatim, animatic 0:30–0:33.)*

Kassey is not shown. No visual reference for him exists yet
(`02_characters/KASSEY_DOSSIER.md`). Whether he and Ada acted willingly or under
coercion on the blast mission, and what the MIC told them the mission was, is open and
not resolved here (`01_canon/OPEN_QUESTIONS.md` #30).

CAPTION: *"The record says dead. The body says no."*

*(Verbatim, animatic 0:33–0:37.)*

ON SCREEN: a cloaked figure cradling another body. Per the locked reference
`assets/concept-art/TALLAI_ADA_RESCUE_REFERENCE_v1.png`: this is **Tallai** — pale
scaled, reptilian skin; a frilled crest along the head; a clawed/webbed hand; a white
care-coat bearing a patch whose text is not legible enough to be canon
(`02_characters/TALLAI_DOSSIER.md`) — tending the wounded Ada, cradling her forehead.

CAPTION: *"Talla calls. Q questions the risk. Quartz moves to carry her."*

*(Verbatim, animatic 0:37–0:45. The animatic's own caption uses "Talla" as a literal
transcript of the artifact; the converged spelling everywhere else is **Tallai**,
per `00_governance/CANON_STATUS.md`.)*

**Q** is present, questioning the risk of the rescue — the only narrative beat on

script does not invent one. **Quartz** moves to physically carry Ada — load-bearer,

(`01_canon/SEED_CANON.md`). No visual reference for Quartz exists yet
(`02_characters/QUARTZ_DOSSIER.md`); this script does not invent one either.

At the very bottom edge of frame, a credit line:

> ADANNA "ADA" VENTER — [role descriptor removed at creator's direction, 2026-06-23],
> OFF T—

Clipped by the source frame's right edge; not recoverable

## Production notes

- This draft's scope matches the animatic's own scope (48.58 seconds of source
  material) expanded into script form — it does not extend the story past what's
  recovered. Whether Episode 1 continues past this point, and with what, is itself an
  open matter, not decided by this script.
- No spoken dialogue exists anywhere in this draft. If Kemi wants characters to speak,
  that's a new draft layer on top of this one, built voice by voice through the
  tiny-questions protocol (`00_governance/TINY_QUESTIONS_PROTOCOL.md`) — starting from
  Ada, who has the most locked personality detail (cold and methodical under pressure)
  of any of the four.
- Visual references used: `assets/concept-art/ADA_CHARACTER_REFERENCE_v1.png`,
  `assets/concept-art/ADA_WASTELAND_REFERENCE_v1.png`,
  `assets/concept-art/TALLAI_ADA_RESCUE_REFERENCE_v1.png`,
  `assets/video/UMADA_Friedmandostorp_Rescue_Animatic.mp4`. Q and Quartz have no visual
  reference yet (`ISSUE-008`, `ISSUE-009`).
- Accessibility: this script is itself a plain-language/text equivalent of the
  animatic's visual sequence, per `00_governance/ACCESSIBILITY_BASELINE.md`. The
  animatic's audio track (score plus any dialogue) still has no transcript
  (`01_canon/OPEN_QUESTIONS.md` #22) — this script cannot supply one, since it covers
  on-screen content only.
