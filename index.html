UMADA

Entry 000 · Welcome mat

This is not a world that was built.

It is a world being remembered.

Some records survived. Some did not. Some refused to disappear.

UMADA is a canon-governed world told as a recovered archive, not a finished script. Every fact here carries a status. Every gap is marked, not filled — if something isn't known yet, the archive says so and waits.

The ground beneath each fact · certainty shown as weight

LOCKED
Confirmed. Will not drift or be quietly swapped for a variant.
PROBABLE
Strongly indicated by source material, not yet fully confirmed.
EMERGING
Named and directional; detail still arriving.
OPEN QUESTION
Genuinely unresolved. Tracked, not guessed at.
RETIRED
A prior error or variant, kept on record so it isn't reintroduced.
AWAITING FRAGMENT
Named but not yet present. The index entry exists; its contents do not, until a fragment arrives.
