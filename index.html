UMADA

This is not a world that was built. It is a world being remembered.

Some records survived. Some did not. Some stories were erased. Some refused to disappear.

If you stay long enough, you may help us remember.

This is the living archive, canon engine, and public site for UMADA — a canon-governed world built from recovered fragments, not invented from nothing. Every fact here carries a status. Every gap is marked, not filled.

Canon status legend

LOCKED PROBABLE EMERGING OPEN QUESTION RETIRED AWAITING FRAGMENT
LOCKED
Confirmed; will not drift or be reintroduced as a variant.
PROBABLE
Strongly indicated by source material, not yet fully confirmed.
EMERGING
Named and directional, detail still arriving.
OPEN QUESTION
Genuinely unresolved — tracked, not guessed at.
RETIRED
A prior error or variant, kept on record so it isn't reintroduced.
AWAITING FRAGMENT
An artifact is named but not yet physically present. The index entry exists; its contents do not, until a fragment arrives.
