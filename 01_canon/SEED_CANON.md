# Seed Canon (consolidated)

Status legend: LOCKED / PROBABLE / EMERGING / OPEN QUESTION / RETIRED

## LOCKED / Near-Locked

- Cape Wipeout, Pt. 1 is the canonical public title.
- Southern Cape // Friedmandostorp Corridor is the corrected geography (near Cape Agulhas; African coastal).
- Rescue Cluster 7-South is a care-based survivor cluster.
- Ada is field commander / scapegoat.
- Ada is listed KIA before final blast confirmation.
- Talla/Tallai is a chimera care specialist.
- Talla/Tallai is known at the bread stop before the blast.
- Bread Sign is an early Nago market sign.
- Q detects. Quartz carries.
- Quartz is load-bearer / rescue anchor.
- ADA-1 begins with body-over-record logic.
- Black Stars Tower hears first.
- Super Hurricane Cindy carried the breach through water, air, and storm.
- Monsters become care infrastructure.

## Friedmandostorp (revised)

- Colorful harbor settlement, weather-rich.
- Southern Cape corridor (near Cape Agulhas); African coastal, never Arctic.
- Bread culture with Nigerian-diaspora Agege-bread influence.
- Origin location of the Bread Sign and Proto-Nago exchange.

## Frame narrative (retained from v1.1)

- UMADA opens through a future reporter / time traveler carrying an Omoluabi device.
- Omoluabi is not the story; it is the device that lets the story be witnessed.
- The future council dispute concerns leadership of Umada and Tisetan.
- Sisekong/Tishekong are transcription errors.
- Opening beat: tick, tick, tick, tick — BOOM.

## Nago canon (from v1.2)

- Nago is the visual operating system of Umada.
- Nago begins as survival vocabulary before grammar.
- Bread Sign is foundational and near-locked.
- Nago must work visually, tactilely, gesturally, verbally, and digitally.
- Nago signs are accessibility infrastructure, not decoration.
- Golden rectangle and ancient geometry provide layout discipline for signage and memory systems.
- Symbol families (function axis): Survival, Care, Signal, Movement, Civic, Memory, Environment.
- Glyph/motif families (narrative axis): Wound, Relay, Archive, Storm, Visitor.
