# Civilizational Ledger
## From Cape Wipeout to Tisetan to Umada

Status: Directional Infrastructure

This ledger gives the UMADA repository a long arc. It is not all locked canon. It tells
the steward how to organize future materials and where the world is heading. The
structured form of these eras also lives in `data/civilizational_ledger.json` and
`data/timeline.json` for use by the site.

## Era 0 — Ancient Memory
Status: PROBABLE

Ancient civilizations rise and fall. Knowledge survives through myth, family transmission, damaged archives, oral memory, and sacred geometry. Atlantis belongs here as remembered catastrophe, not generic fantasy.

## Era 1 — The Hidden Archive
Status: PROBABLE

Human history is not alone. Alien presence exists as fragmented evidence, suppressed record, misunderstood myth, and hidden archive.

## Era 2 — S-Vector
Status: PROBABLE

S-Vector connects biology, technology, memory, adaptation, consciousness, and old experiments. No civilization fully understands it.

## Era 3 — Chimera Research
Status: LOCKED / EMERGING

Human institutions conduct experimentation for medicine, adaptation, military advantage, and survival. Chimeras emerge as new beings, not monsters.

## Era 4 — The Garden
Status: LOCKED

The Garden represents coexistence, curiosity, care, and possibility. Tallai explores the Garden.

## Era 5 — Cape Wipeout
Status: LOCKED

March 19, 2226. Near Cape Agulhas. Chimeran Flu outbreak. Military Industrial Complex destroys facility. Official story diverges from truth. Ada, Tallai, Q, and Quartz survive.

## Era 6 — The Breach
Status: EMERGING

Containment fails. The Atlantic becomes a carrier. The world changes.

## Era 7 — Super Hurricane Cindy
Status: EMERGING

Cindy carries the breach through water, air, and storm. Signal and contagion intertwine.

## Era 8 — Angel Dust
Status: EMERGING

Chimeran Flu evolves. Angel Dust emerges. The world enters prolonged instability.

## Era 9 — Emergency Cybernetics
Status: EMERGING

Cybernetics begin as medical repair, accessibility, and survival tools. Later they become status systems.

## Era 10 — Dead Glass
Status: LOCKED / EMERGING

Network collapse. Infrastructure collapse. Information collapse. Civilizations reorganize around new communication and survival systems.

## Era 11 — The Relays
Status: EMERGING

Hand Relay expands. Local signs spread. Community signals emerge. Language becomes infrastructure.

## Era 12 — Nago Standardization
Status: EMERGING

Early survival signs evolve into civic/accessibility/memory infrastructure. Bread, Water, Safe, Carry, Home, and Danger become foundations.

## Era 13 — The Tower
Status: EMERGING

The Black Stars Tower hears first. Listening becomes infrastructure. The Black Stars
themselves are the premier scientific civilization of the future, remembered for
transforming humanity's ability to observe reality rather than for military power
(Kemi, 2026-06-25; full profile: `01_canon/BLACK_STARS_CIVILIZATION.md`). Where their
deep-space exploration era — "The Kuiper Belt Renaissance" — falls relative to this Era
and Era 15 below is not yet specified; see `01_canon/OPEN_QUESTIONS.md`.

## Era 14 — Tisetan Rising
Status: EMERGING

Tisetan rises over the wound. It becomes signal city, relay city, accessibility city, and archive city.

## Era 15 — The Kingdom Age
Status: EMERGING

Multiple powers emerge: Lion Kingdom, Jackal Peoples, Black Stars, Phoenix Rising, Cybernetic Houses, Pure Human Communities.

## Era 16 — Umada
Status: EMERGING

Umada becomes a place and an idea: civilization organized around care, memory, access, witness, and repair.
