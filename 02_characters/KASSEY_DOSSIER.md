# Kassey — Character Dossier

Status: **EMERGING** (confirmed facts below) / **DRAFT — open fields awaiting Kemi**

Kassey enters canon through Ada: first as a name burned across her military record in the
rescue animatic, then confirmed by Kemi (2026-06-23) as a person in his own right. This
dossier records only what Kemi and the realized animatic confirm. Appearance, voice, role,
and history are open fields, not guesses — per the prime directive, imaginative content is
Kemi's to draft.

## Identity

- **Name:** Kassey. Canonical written form **Kassey** (the spelling burned into the
  rescue animatic, the visual source of truth); **KC** is the short form.
- **A separate person from Ada** (confirmed by Kemi, 2026-06-23) — not another name for
  Ada herself.

## Confirmed facts

- **Ada's lover** — a man, romantically partnered with Adanna "Ada" Venter (Kemi,
  2026-06-23).
- **Gave Ada her necklace** (Kemi, 2026-06-23): a simple silver chain with a small, solid
  heart pendant (not a locket). What it means to Ada is open — see
  `02_characters/ADA_DOSSIER.md`.
- His name **"Kassey" burns across Ada's torn military record** in the Friedmandostorp
  rescue animatic, in a different, glowing script from the typed record (March 19, 2226).
  Source: `01_canon/VISUAL_CANON_REGISTRY.md`. Note: the record itself — stamped CLEARANCE
  DENIED / KIA and tagged "Chimera Survivor. Scientist. Empath." — is **Ada's** record
  (Adanna K. Venter); those descriptors are hers, not Kassey's. Only his name crosses it.
- **Alive** through Cape Wipeout (Kemi, 2026-06-23) — he is not the figure listed KIA;
  that is Ada's record.
- **Sent by the Military Industrial Complex (MIC), together with Ada, to execute the Cape
  Wipeout blast** — the March 19, 2226 detonation that obliterates the facility (Kemi,
  2026-06-23). This elaborates the already-locked canon that the MIC destroys the facility:
  Ada and Kassey were the operatives it used.
