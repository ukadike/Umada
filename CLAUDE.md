## Prime directive

Kemi's job is imagination. Your job is documentation, classification, continuity,

## No fabrication

Never invent canon. Unknowns become `OPEN QUESTION`, `AWAITING FRAGMENT`, or one tiny
question. For any artifact not physically present in the repository, create an index
entry only, tagged `AWAITING FRAGMENT`; do not describe, summarize, or infer its contents.

## Frame narrative

UMADA opens through a future reporter / time traveler carrying an Omoluabi device.
Omoluabi is not the story; it is the device that lets the story be witnessed. The future
council dispute concerns leadership of **Umada and Tisetan**. Sisekong/Tishekong are
transcription errors. Opening beat: tick, tick, tick, tick, tick — BOOM, visualized as
an explosion on screen.

## Nago Symbology

Nago is the visual operating system of Umada — survival language before grammar,
accessibility infrastructure, tactile signage, civic memory, relay/signal system,
market-originated communication, later civilizational symbol system. Never generic
decorative glyphs (see `NAGO_NO_GENERIC_GLYPHS_RULE.md`).

Two axes — do not merge:
- **Function families (signage):** Survival, Care, Signal, Movement, Civic, Memory, Environment.
- **Motif families (narrative):** Wound, Relay, Archive, Storm, Visitor.

Canonical core survival signs (locked to the realized plate): Bread, Water, Safe,
Danger, Carry, Home, Help, Rest. The realized plates in `assets/img/` are the visual
source of truth; where docs and plates disagree, the plates win. "Nago" carries a
real-world provenance note (Nagô/Yoruba) — see the Symbology Bible.

Every Nago sign needs: name, canon status, gesture, meaning, visual mark, tactile form,
use context, accessibility notes, provenance/care notes, open questions.

## Visual layer split

- Presentation/archive layer: monochrome graphite archive (paper #efece2, ink #1a1a1a,
  line #d0cdc6, muted #6f6f6f; Helvetica Neue / Georgia / Courier Prime).
- Diegetic world layer: colorful when canon supports it. The world is southern-African
  coastal (Southern Cape corridor, near Cape Agulhas) — colorful, weather-rich, painted.
  Never Arctic. Real-world palette references stay internal mood notes, never named in
  canon or public materials.

## Accessibility

Accessibility is infrastructure: alt text, screen-reader labels, keyboard access,
no color-only meaning, no hover-only meaning, reduced motion, plain-language summaries,
and tactile/sign equivalents when relevant.

## Tiny questions

Ask one tiny question at a time, only when a draft cannot proceed. Draft first.

## Fanbase / Faraday Box

Fans contribute signal, not automatic canon. The Faraday Box receives audience input,
protects canon, filters harm, preserves traces, and returns prompts.
