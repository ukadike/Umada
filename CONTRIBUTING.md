# Contributing

UMADA takes two kinds of contribution, and they go through different doors.

## Production contributors

Writers, artists, accessibility reviewers, and researchers doing production work:
start at [`docs/CONTRIBUTOR_GUIDE.md`](docs/CONTRIBUTOR_GUIDE.md), and read
[`CLAUDE.md`](CLAUDE.md) and [`00_governance/NO_FABRICATION_RULE.md`](00_governance/NO_FABRICATION_RULE.md)
first. New source material is ingested through
[`00_governance/FRAGMENT_WORKFLOW.md`](00_governance/FRAGMENT_WORKFLOW.md), never
committed directly into `01_canon/`.

## Fans

Fans contribute signal, not automatic canon, through the Faraday Box:
[`16_fanbase/FANBASE_FARADAY_BOX.md`](16_fanbase/FANBASE_FARADAY_BOX.md) or the live
page at [`sections/faraday-box.html`](sections/faraday-box.html). See
[`18_fan_archive/FAN_ARCHIVE.md`](18_fan_archive/FAN_ARCHIVE.md) for how contributions
and development history are preserved.
