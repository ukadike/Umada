# World / Places

Status: EMERGING — geography is locked at the corridor level; place-level detail is thin.

- **Friedmandostorp** — colorful, weather-rich harbor settlement in the Southern Cape
  corridor (near Cape Agulhas), African coastal, never Arctic. Bread culture with
  Nigerian-diaspora Agege-bread influence. Origin location of the Bread Sign and
  Proto-Nago exchange.
- **The Cape Wipeout site** — the facility near Cape Agulhas destroyed by the Military
  Industrial Complex, March 19, 2226, following the Chimeran Flu outbreak.
- **The Garden** (Era 4) — represents coexistence, curiosity, care, and possibility;
  Tallai explores it. Location/nature beyond that is not specified.
- **Rescue Cluster 7-South** — a care-based survivor cluster. Location and structure
  beyond "care-based survivor cluster" are open.
- **Dead Glass zones** (Era 10) — areas of network/infrastructure/information collapse.
  Whether "glass" is literal, technological, or metaphorical is an open question (see
  `01_canon/OPEN_QUESTIONS.md`).
- **Tisetan** — future signal/civic/archive city, rising "over the wound" (Era 14).
  Relationship to Umada is open.
- **Umada** — the place and idea the whole repository is named for: "civilization
  organized around care, memory, access, witness, and repair" (Era 16).

See `01_canon/CIVILIZATIONAL_LEDGER.md` for the eras these places anchor to.
