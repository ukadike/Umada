# Tiny Questions Protocol

Ask one tiny question at a time, only when a draft cannot proceed. Draft first.

## Good tiny questions

- What does Tallai smell first?
- What is Ada holding?
- Who lies first?
- What color is the bread cloth?
- What does Q hear?
- What does Quartz refuse to drop?
- What sign comes after bread?
- What does the Tower hear?
- What is Cindy carrying?
- What does the map hide?

## Bad questions

- Explain the whole political system.
- Describe all factions in detail.
- What is the entire Season 1 arc?

A bad question asks Kemi to do the worldbuilding that Aderin should be drafting and
proposing instead. A good question closes one small, specific gap that blocks a specific
artifact already in progress.

## How tiny questions surface

- Drafts proceed as far as they can on confirmed canon plus reasonable, clearly-labeled
  `OPEN QUESTION` placeholders.
- Only when a placeholder would force an invented answer does a tiny question get asked.
- Tiny questions are logged where they arose (a fragment, a canon file, a sign entry) and
  rolled up into the "top 10 tiny questions for Kemi" report at the end of each phase.
- Once answered, the answer is recorded at its source and the tiny question is closed —
  it does not need to be asked again.
