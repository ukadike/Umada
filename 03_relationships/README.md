# Relationships

Status: EMERGING — most relationships are still unlocked beyond the survivor unit and
the partnerships below.

Known: Ada, Tallai/Talla, Q, and Quartz are bound together as the four survivors of Cape
Wipeout. "Q detects. Quartz carries." names a functional pairing between two of them, not
a personal relationship.

**Ada & KC (added 2026-06-25, per `UMADA_CANON_INDEX.md`):** KC is Ada's partner. Both
are Cape-operation operatives; their relationship is also an ethical counterpoint — KC
accepts limited restorative cybernetics but refuses full restructuring, in explicit
contrast to Ada's total cybernetic reconstruction. KC is present at the Cape Operation,
meant to meet Ada at the Friedmandostorp bread stand, and is "separated" (not confirmed
killed) at the moment of Cape Wipeout. See `01_canon/OPEN_QUESTIONS.md` (#30) for whether
this should ever change KC's status relative to the locked four-survivor unit.

**Ada & Tallai (per `UMADA_CANON_INDEX.md`):** rescuer, companion, and later estate
steward — Tallai is the first being to discover Ada alive after Cape Wipeout and later
becomes Steward of Ada's Estate of Memory.

**Ada & the Reporter (per `UMADA_CANON_INDEX.md`):** the Reporter is chosen by Ada for
the time-investigation task; Ada herself refuses to become the time traveler.

No other relationship (familial, rivalrous, mentorship) is documented. This directory
holds relationship maps once fragments establish them — see
`00_governance/FRAGMENT_WORKFLOW.md`.
