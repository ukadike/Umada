# World Overview

Status: EMERGING — geography is locked at the corridor level; place-level detail beyond
that is thin. Full detail: `05_world/README.md` and `01_canon/CIVILIZATIONAL_LEDGER.md`.
This page is a production-facing summary, not a new source of facts.

## Geography (LOCKED)

The world is **southern-African coastal** — the Southern Cape corridor, near Cape
Agulhas. **Never Arctic** — no ice, snow, glacier, or Nordic/Greenland framing, ever;
this was a reintroduced error in a prior revision and is permanently retired (see
`00_governance/CANON_STATUS.md`).

## Known places

- **Friedmandostorp** — colorful, weather-rich harbor settlement in the corridor, with
  Nigerian-diaspora Agege-bread influence. Origin location of the Bread Sign and
  Proto-Nago exchange.
- **The Cape Wipeout site** — the facility near Cape Agulhas destroyed by the Military
  Industrial Complex, March 19, 2226, following the Chimeran Flu outbreak.
- **The Garden** (Era 4) — coexistence, curiosity, care, and possibility; Tallai
  explores it. Location and physical nature beyond that: `[TBD]`.
- **Rescue Cluster 7-South** — a care-based survivor cluster; structure beyond that is
  open.
- **Dead Glass zones** (Era 10) — network/infrastructure/information collapse; whether
  "glass" is literal, technological, or metaphorical is an open question.
- **Tisetan** — future signal/civic/archive city, rising "over the wound" (Era 14).
- **Umada** — the place and idea the repository is named for: "civilization organized
  around care, memory, access, witness, and repair" (Era 16).
