# Nago Signage Guide v1

Status: EMERGING

## Purpose

Nago signage is public infrastructure.

It appears in:

- markets
- bread stops
- clinics
- water points
- rescue routes
- towers
- archives
- schools
- transit corridors
- Dead Glass zones
- Tisetan
- Umada

### Accessibility Signs

- Tactile route

## Public Sign Template

Each sign should include:

- Nago mark
- plain-language label
- tactile raised mark
- directional arrow if needed
- screen-reader equivalent
- local language equivalents
- warning if high-risk
- provenance note

## Rule

No sign may rely only on color.

No sign may require perfect vision.

No sign may require literacy alone.

No sign may require hearing alone.

Nago is multimodal by default.

See `00_governance/ACCESSIBILITY_BASELINE.md` for how this guide's rules apply site-wide.
