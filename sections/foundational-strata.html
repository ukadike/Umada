Foundational Strata

Ten layers UMADA is built on, each a lens the rest of the archive is read through.

  1. Care Doctrine — Extraction, Stabilization, Care, Analysis, Transport, Witness.
  2. Accessibility Civilization — Access = Survival.
  3. Signal Theory — Storms, stories, language, disease, memory, towers, relays.
  4. Nago Origins — Bread sign, market signs, survival vocabulary before grammar.
  5. Friedmandostorp — Bread stop, market, Southern Cape corridor, weather, harbor settlement, Nigerian-diaspora Agege-bread influence.
  6. Body Before Record — the ADA-1 principle: trust body before record; trust witness before narrative; trust survival before authority.
  7. Monsters as Care Infrastructure — chimeras become the care institutions refused to give.
  8. Hidden Archive — aliens, Atlantis, S-Vector, damaged records, missing evidence.
  9. SSL Layer — UMADA as the mythological/narrative layer of Small Systems Lab.
  10. The Tower — the Black Stars Tower hears first. Listening is infrastructure.
