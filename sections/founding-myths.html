Founding Myths and First Lines

Poetry is a first-class canon source in UMADA. These lines are preserved verbatim; canon facts are extracted from them separately rather than paraphrased over them.

Tick.
Tick.
Tick.
Tick.

BOOM.

The Black Stars Tower hears first.

We do not leave ours.

Trust body before record.
Trust witness before narrative.
Trust survival before authority.

They called us monsters.
We became the care they were afraid to give.

Omoluabi is not the story.
It is the device that lets the story be witnessed.

A sign is a bridge.
A bridge is a promise.
A promise is a survival.

Super Hurricane Cindy carried the breach through water, air, and storm.

Source: 01_canon/FOUNDING_MYTHS_AND_FIRST_LINES.md. Canon extracted from these lines lives in 01_canon/SEED_CANON.md and data/canon.json.
