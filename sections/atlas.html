Atlas

UMADA's geography is locked at the corridor level. No canonical map exists yet — that is recorded as an open question rather than filled in.

Locked geography

  • The world is southern-African coastal — Southern Cape corridor, near Cape Agulhas.
  • Friedmandostorp is a colorful, weather-rich harbor settlement with Nigerian-diaspora Agege-bread influence.
  • Never Arctic. Any Arctic, Greenland, or Nordic framing is permanently retired — see 00_governance/CANON_STATUS.md.

Open question

OPEN QUESTION No canonical map artifact of Friedmandostorp, the Southern Cape corridor, Tisetan, or Umada is currently present in this repository. This atlas section will hold one once a map fragment arrives — see 00_governance/FRAGMENT_WORKFLOW.md.
