# Fanbase + Faraday Box Layer

## Fan thesis

Welcome to UMADA.

This is not a world that was built.
It is a world being remembered.

Some records survived.
Some did not.

Some stories were erased.
Some stories refused to disappear.

If you stay long enough, you may help us remember.

## Participation rule

Fans contribute signal, not automatic canon.

Audience contributions pass through the Faraday Box.

## Faraday Box

The Faraday Box:

- receives audience signal
- protects canon
- filters harm
- preserves audience traces
- returns prompts
- distinguishes fan signal from canon

## Fan prompts

- What sign would your community create?
- What does care look like after collapse?
- What memory should never be erased?
- What would your relay mark be?
- What should a tower listen for?
- What does accessibility look like in your future city?
- What does your community carry?
- What story would power try to erase?
